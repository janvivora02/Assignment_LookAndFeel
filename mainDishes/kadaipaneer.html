Kadai Paneer

Ingredients
  • 1 tbsp Ghee
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • 1 tsp Ginger Paste
  • 1 tsp garlic Paste
  • 1 Capsicum (Diced)
  • 1 Onion (Diced)
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tbsp Red Chilli Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala Powder
  • 2 cups Water
  • Salt to Taste
  • 1/2 Cup Fresh Cream
  • 1 kg Paneer

Method:

Paneer Kadai is a restaurant-style cottage cheese recipe. Read and learn how to make it at home

Ingredients For Garam Masala :

  • 3 tbsp Coriander Seeds
  • 1 tbsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1 1/2 tsp Fennel Seeds
  • 1/2 tsp Cloves
  • 6 Green Cardamom (Seeds)
  • 1/2 tsp Pepper Corns
  • 1 Bay Leaf
Method for Garam Masala
  1. Add all these ingredients in a pan.
  2. Dry roast it till they leave nice aroma.
  3. Grind the masala into a fine powder.
Tomato Onion paste ingredients:
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • 4 finely chopped Onions
  • 4 finely chopped Tomatoes
Method for Tomato Onion paste :
  1. In a Kadai, add 2 tbsp oil.
  2. Once the oil heats up, add the chopped onions and saute it till it becomes translucent.
  3. Once the Onions are soft, add the chopped tomatoes and cook it will its mushy.
  4. Once the onions and tomatoes are soft, shut the flame and let it cool.
  5. Transfer these into a grinder and grind it into a fine paste.
Gravy Method :
  1. In a pan add 1 tbsp butter and 1 tbsp Ghee
  2. Once it's hot add 1 tsp Ginger and 1 tsp Garlic and saute it till the raw smell goes off.
  3. Once, the ginger garlic is cooked, add 1 capsicum and 1 Onion.
  4. Saute it till it gets soft and cooked.
  5. After it's cooked, add the Onion Tomato Paste and let it cook for 2-3 mins
  6. Add 1/2 tsp turmeric,
  7. 1 tbsp Red Chilli Powder
  8. 1 tbsp Garam Masala
  9. Mix everything together.
  10. Add 2 cups of Water and Salt to taste
  11. Give it a Stir and cook it till the Capsicum are cooked.
  12. Lower the flame and Add 1/2 Cup Cream, Mix well and let it boil for 3-4 min.
  13. Add 1 kg Paneer Cubes and mix it gently
  14. Cover and cook for more 2-3 mins
Your Kadai Paneer is ready to be served, you can have it with Kulcha and Naan.
