Chettinad Fish Curry

 Instructions
                               
                                
Chettinad Fish Curry:
                               
Chettinad style fish curry recipe is native to the Chettinad region of Southern India. 
This delicious curry recipe is simple and easy to make and can be relished with hot steamed rice.

Ingredients 
11 pods of garlic
2 onions, chopped
1 tomato, chopped
1 inch piece of ginger
1 cup freshly shredded coconut
1 tsp fennel seeds
2 tsp coriander seeds
1 tsp mustard seeds
2-3 green chillies
Tamarind soaked in water
2 pieces of Indian king fish
Coconut oil

Method 

For masala
In a pan add coconut oil, 3-4 cloves of garlic, 1/2 inch of ginger, 1 chopped onion, 1 chopped tomato and cook it until the onions turn brown.

In a blender add the cooked onions and tomato, 1 cup shredded coconut, 1 tsp of fennel seeds, 2 tsp of coriander seeds and blend it into thick paste.

For curry
In a pan add coconut oil, 1 tsp mustard seeds, 6-7 pods of garlic, 1/2 inch of ginger, 2-3 slit green chillies, curry leaves, 1 chopped onion, salt, ground masala and give them a mix.

Add 1/2 tsp turmeric powder, 2 tsp of red chilli powder, tamarind water and mix it well.

Fill the pan half with water, add some coriander leaves and allow it to cook for 8-10 mins.

Add desired pieces of Fish and cook it for next 5-7 mins on low flame.

Chettinad Fish Curry is ready to be served!
